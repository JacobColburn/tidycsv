FAQ

Is my CSV data uploaded anywhere?

No. The conversion happens entirely in your browser with JavaScript. Your file is never sent to a server, which makes TidyCSV safe for customer lists, exports with personal data, and other sensitive files.

Does it handle semicolon-separated or tab-separated files?

Yes. The delimiter is auto-detected (comma, semicolon, tab, or pipe), and you can override it manually. Quoted fields, embedded commas, and line breaks inside cells are handled per the CSV standard (RFC 4180).

What JSON format do I get?

With "First row is headers" checked you get an array of objects keyed by column name. Unchecked, you get an array of arrays — one array per row.

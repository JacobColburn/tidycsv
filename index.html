Why local-first?

Most online converters upload your file to their server, process it there, and promise to delete it later. CSV files are usually the sensitive kind — customer lists, employee data, financial exports. TidyCSV takes a different approach: the code runs in your browser, so there is nothing to upload and nothing for us to delete. You can disconnect from the internet after the page loads and every tool keeps working.

Free forever?

The tools on this page are free with no row limits. More tools are on the way — merge, column picker, and batch processing.

FAQ

Is my data uploaded anywhere?

No. Deduplication happens entirely in your browser with JavaScript — nothing is sent to a server. That makes it safe for mailing lists, customer exports, and other files with personal data.

How are duplicates detected?

Two rows are duplicates when every cell matches exactly. Turn on "Ignore case" to also match rows that differ only in capitalization, and "Ignore extra whitespace" to match rows that differ only by leading or trailing spaces. The first occurrence is always kept, and row order is preserved.

Does it work with semicolon or tab-separated files?

Yes — the delimiter is auto-detected (comma, semicolon, tab, or pipe) and preserved in the output.
